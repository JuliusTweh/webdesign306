COMPANY PROFILE

Tweh & Son's Inc. was established in the eighties under the professed management and administration of Mr. Julius Tweh. Initially Tweh and Son's Inc.   had started out as a small retail wholesale stationery outlet. During the years the Tweh and Son's Inc.   store emerged as core business in office supplies industry. Tweh & Son's Inc. is committed to customer value, our team being operate responsibly to differentiate us from other companies. We think our beloved clients its patrons will find our story to be a unique one. We owe our achievements to God's grace and the loyal patronage of our beloved customers, who have supported and believed in us throughout the years.
